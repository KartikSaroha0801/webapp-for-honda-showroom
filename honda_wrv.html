Honda WR-V Summary


The WR-V was first showcased in the Indian market in 2017. Honda later updated the car to meet BS6 emission norms in 2020 and it then also came with some added features. This car is available in six exterior paints - Platinum White Pearl, Radiant Red Metallic, Golden Brown Metallic, Lunar Silver Metallic, Premium Amber Metallic and Modern Steel Metallic. This is a sub-four metre car and has a 188mm ground clearance. The updated WR-V comes with redesigned front and rear. There is a new grille with a chrome bar. It also gets new LED projector headlamps, LED DRLs and LED fog lamps. Besides, the WR-V features body cladding across the lowermost body panels. There are new elements inside the tail lamp unit such as the J-shaped LED taillamps and 16-inch alloy wheels too.


Honda WR-V Price

Honda WR-V price starts at ₹ 9.02 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 12.18 Lakh (Avg. ex-showroom). WR-V comes in 4 variants. The price of WR-V petrol top version is ₹ 10.03 Lakh. The price of WR-V diesel base version is ₹ 11.17 Lakh.
